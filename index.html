About Me

Hi, welcome to my webpage! I am a second-year physics Ph.D. student at Old Dominion University in Norfolk, VA, working on various research projects through the Jefferson Lab theory center. In Spring 2023, I graduated from Wichita State University with a Bachelor's degree in physics and math along with a minor in computer science. I possess a curiosity that is seldom satisfied and drives my passion for learning about and exploring the world around me.
